How to Play:

  • To start, click the 'Roll the Dice' button.
  • To keep the rounds going, click on 'Roll the Dice' again.
  • After 3 rounds, the game is over and the score is calculated.
  • If you want to play again, click the 'Start Over/Reset' button or refresh your page.

Rules:

  • If any of your two dice comes up as 1, the total score would = 0 for the round.
  • Roll doubles (ex. 5 & 5) to have your score multiplied by 2!
  • Roll any other combination, and the sum gets added together.
  • Player who has the most points at the end, wins.
